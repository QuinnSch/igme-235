Description

The Binding of Isaac: Repentance is the most recent DLC expansion to the popular roguelike game, The Binding of Isaac: Rebirth. It adds an entire new set of floors to explore, all filled with new enemies, bosses and endings. Along with the new challenges comes an entire set of additional items and characters to play as. All the new content in this expansion easily adds a hundred hours of replayability.
